Games of 2023

First half of the year was "games as usual". Cesspool was a very indie turn-based RPG. Pretty buggy at the time, but chock-full of heart. I actually used my Twitch stream videos to log some bugs. I look forward to coming back to it sometime.

I got a Switch and also a capture device so I could stream games from it! I played through Super Mario Odyssey, which was a solid mario game. I was only slightly disappointed in the music, which wasn't the orchestral masterpiece that was Super Mario Galaxy.

Loop Hero was the first roguelike I've seen to the end! Very creative and eventually a bit addictive. Thanks to my sis for the recommendation!

And, yeah...not much gaming in the latter half of the year. What can I say, break-ups are hard...

I streamed most games I played AND archived the full playthroughs, so feel free to check them out!
