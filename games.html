My video games

Last updated: Mar 30, 2024

I received my first video game, Super Mario Bros. / Duck Hunt, with my Nintendo when I was merely seven years old. Ever since then, lots of my hard-earned cash has gone towards the purchase of many more games for several consoles. There are bigger, more impressive video game collections out there, but I'm proud of mine nonetheless.

Check out my Twitch channel if you want to see me play some games.
